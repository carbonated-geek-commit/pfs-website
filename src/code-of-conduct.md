<div class="container prose">

Peninsula Fermentation Society (PFS) is dedicated to providing a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of gender, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, race or religion. We do not tolerate harassment of its members in any form. All communication should be appropriate for people of diverse backgrounds and cultures. Sexual language and imagery is not appropriate.

Be kind to others. Do not insult or defame other members and their guests. Harassment in any form — sexist, racist, or exclusionary jokes — is not condoned.

All participants in PFS activities are expected to conduct themselves with integrity, courtesy and respect for others. Members and/or guests violating these rules may be asked to leave the event and may be banned from participating in future PFS activities at the sole discretion of the PFS Executive Committee. Thanks for helping to make this a welcoming experience for all.

By participating in PFS activities, you agree to follow and uphold this code of conduct.
