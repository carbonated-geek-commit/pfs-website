About

The Society

Founded in 2023, the Peninsula Fermentation Society exists to share knowledge of the craft and science of fermenting, build our skills as brewers and fermenters, and foster camaraderie around fermented beverages of every kind — beer, cider, mead, and beyond.

3 · Implementation Notes

Measurement setup

One GA4 property, tag in the site <head>. Mark join_click and contact_email_click as key events. IP anonymization on; no ads features needed for a club site.

Document security

Move Drive folders behind a Google Group ("members"). The library pages link to files via the group-restricted share, so downloads require sign-in and folder metadata (owner emails, revision history) stays private.

Calendar

Replace the embedded Google Calendar iframe with a published read-only calendar + an .ics subscribe link, so no personal account is exposed and members can add meetings to their own calendars.
